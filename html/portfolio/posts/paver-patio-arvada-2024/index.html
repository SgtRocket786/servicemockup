Paver Patio — Arvada, CO

Completed: 2024
Category: Patios & Hardscaping
Location: Arvada, CO

Project Overview

This paver patio project transformed a small backyard into a functional outdoor living space with custom pavers, seating walls, and integrated lighting. The design focuses on durability and low maintenance while keeping a warm, natural aesthetic.

Before

The yard had a compacted lawn and poor drainage. These photos show the site prior to excavation and base preparation — uneven grade and limited usable outdoor living area.

After

After installing a compacted base and setting the pavers, the space now supports dining, a fire pit, and integrated lighting for evening use. The new layout increases usable square footage and improves drainage.
