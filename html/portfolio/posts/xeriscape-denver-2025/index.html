Xeriscape Refresh — Denver, CO

Completed: 2025
Category: Xeriscape
Location: Denver, CO

Project Overview

A drought-tolerant front yard remodel that uses native plants, permeable pathways, and efficient irrigation to create a beautiful low-water landscape with year-round interest.

Before

The original front yard relied on high-water turf and had limited biodiversity. These images show the area before renovation.

After

The renovated xeriscape includes native grasses, drought-tolerant perennials, and a new drip irrigation system. The result is a low maintenance yard that still offers seasonal color and curb appeal.
